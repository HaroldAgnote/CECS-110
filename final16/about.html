Computer Science

What is Computer Science?

In a general definition, Computer Science can be defined as the study of Computer Systems in where applications and programs are designed in order to complete tasks.

Why do we need Computer Science?

Computer Science is especially important in our developing society due to the fact that it is used to solve problems and make tasks easier to complete. Furthermore, it allows us to utilize technology effectively in a more modern society and easily network people together.

How do we use Computer Science?

There are countless ways in which we use Computer Science. Some languages can include: Java, C++, Python, HTML/CSS, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, F#, etc.
The list goes on and on...Computer Science can be used to build websites, make games, network people together, build robots and artificial intelligence.
The usages of Computer Science is evergrowing, and its future is certainly bright.
